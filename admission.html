We offer 6 bachelor course i.e. Bachelors of Business Administration (BBA), Bachelors of Information Management (BIM), Bachelors of Business Studies (BBS), Bachelors of Computer Applications (BCA), Bachelors of Business Management (BBM), BSc. Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc. CSIT), and a master's course i.e. Masters of Business Studies (MBS)

Students should attend and pass the CMAT entrance exam conducted by TU to be eligible to apply for BBA / BIM / BBM.

Students should attend and pass the IOST entrance exam conducted by TU to be eligible to apply for BSc. CSIT.

Students should attend and pass the BCA entrance exam conducted by TU to be eligible to apply for BCA.
